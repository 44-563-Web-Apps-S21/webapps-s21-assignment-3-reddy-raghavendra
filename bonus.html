The White Anaconda

The White Anaconda is the arch nemesis of Kōjōman, he attacks his opponents with venomous bites, his ultimate offensive technique is the eight branch technique an ability which allows him to transform into a massive eight headed snake - causing him to transcend from a Snake to Dragon God, with him appearing inside the mouth of the primary snake head.

Lair of the White Anaconda

  1. Streams
  2. Rivers
  3. Swamps
  4. marshes
  5. large trees
