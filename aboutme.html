Kōjōman

Kakashi Kōjō(Kōjōman) belongs to Kōjō village. He is a child prodigy of the Kōjō clan.He has an ability to interact with
all kind of plants and also to control them if needed. He is one of the survivors of the Kōjō village war which took place when
he was only 10. This war was between Orochimaru's army and the Kōjō village as the village was against the criminal ideas of orochimaru which was to seek immortality.
Kakashi since the war, his only aim is to destroy Orochimaru and his army. For this goal he is continously training himself and making allies to destroy him.
